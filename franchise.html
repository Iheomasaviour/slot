SLOT Systems Limited is a leading Nigerian technology retail chain company founded in 1998.

We specialize in the sales and distribution of mobile phones, consumer electronics, mobile & IT accessories, computing devices, and a wide range of gadgets.

With over 80 stores nationwide, SLOT has grown into a household name in Nigeria, renowned for offering a wide range of tech products from globally recognized brands such as Samsung, Apple, Tecno, Xiaomi, HP, Lenovo, Asus, Dell, LG, Hisense, and more.

SLOT is also known for its commitment to providing unequalled customer service, reliable after-sales support, and a seamless shopping experience online and offline.
